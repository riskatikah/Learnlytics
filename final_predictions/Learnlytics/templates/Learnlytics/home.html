Learnlytics

Learnlytics is a web-based data science application designed to analyze and predict student academic performance based on their learning activities. This project utilizes data such as grade history, participation in quizzes, assignments, and discussion forums to provide insights for students, lecturers, and academic administrators. The target users of this application include university students, faculty members, and campus management who aim to understand and improve academic outcomes through data-driven approaches.
